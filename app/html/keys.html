• Contacts
  • - You can assign a PGP Public Key to each of your contacts, which will be used in the case that you want to send them an encrypted email.


  • Personal Key Pair
  •   or  
  • - To import or create a PGP Keypair, you will need a passphrase, which will be used to encrypt your private key. If you forget your passphrase, your Keypair will be rendered useless.
  • - Once you create or import your PGP Keypair, you will have the option to include your Public Key as an attachment inside the emails that you send.
  • - Your Private Key will be used when you want to decrypt a message that was sent to you and was encrypted with your Public Key mentioned above, as well as to sign the emails that you send.
